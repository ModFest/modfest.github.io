Welcome to ModFest 1.14!

ModFest 1.14 was a Fabric modding event that ran from April 20th, 2019 to April 27th, 2019.

Requirements:

  • Minecraft 1.14
  • Fabric Loader
  • Mods must be created roughly between April 20th and April 27th

Info:

  • No Voting
  • No Pressure
  • Just Make Mods

After this ModFest was conducted, one mod was released.
