Welcome to ModFest 1.15!

ModFest 1.15 is a Fabric modding event that ran from March 28th to April 5th.

Requirements:

  • Minecraft 1.15.2
  • Fabric Loader
  • Mods must be created between March 28th and April 5th
  • Mods are encouraged to use one of the Themes

You can work:

  • Solo (By Yourself)
  • With a Custom Team
  • With a Randomized Team

Themes:

  • Exploration
  • Industry
  • Magic

At the end of ModFest 1.15, 39 mods were released.
