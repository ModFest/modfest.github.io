ModFest 1.16 Awards

Note that each mod is listed in alphabetical order in each category.

Adventure Theme Award:

Labyrinthine

by FoundationGames and Yaku

Adds a dimension with hidden treasure, locked away behind a tricky maze.

Astromine

by vini2003, MoonlitLamps, Shnupbups, Draylar, SuperCoder79, AK9, HalfOf2, and shedaniel

A space-themed technological mod with exploration and progression in mind.

PolyDungeons

by Earthcomputer, soap, MarkerToPaper, and DiamondMCPro

A combat-focused dungeon adventure.

Fantasy Theme Award:

Haema

by Will BL

Lacrimis

Mycoturgy

by B0undarybreaker
